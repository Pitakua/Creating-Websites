Huarache

Clan

"The Huarache Clan is a collective of and community organizers who help indigenous communities and their rights. They are a grassroots organization that is dedicated to help them in every possible way, wether is by learning how to read, speak english, dance, understand the laws and their rights, ect. Most importantly, they are proud of wearing their huaraches which translates to sandals"
